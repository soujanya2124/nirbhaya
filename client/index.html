Frequently Asked Questions

How does the app work?

Nirbhaya uses your phone's location services to connect you with nearby emergency services and your trusted contacts. With just one tap, you can activate the SOS feature and get immediate assistance.

Is it available worldwide?

Currently, Nirbhaya is available in select regions and we're rapidly expanding our coverage to ensure women's safety worldwide.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, Nirbhaya is completely free to use. Our mission is to make safety accessible to all women without any barriers.
